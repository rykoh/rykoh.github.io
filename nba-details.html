For my statistics final project, the professor provided us the opportunity to analyze any set of data. This was shortly after the 2019-2020 NBA was canceled. Being a huge basketball fan (Go Houston Rockets!), it was the perfect opportunity to explore potential outcomes for the season. The most reasonable way to do this would be using Elo ratings, a common ranking system used in chess and video games. Using Elo ratings for the teams, I could then simulate each game in the remaining schedule using a random generator based on probability distributions centered around a teams Elo. This rating would then be updated to reflect the games they win/lose. Overall, the simulation was quite accurately, ultimately correctly predicting the season's NBA champions, the Los Angeles Lakers.
