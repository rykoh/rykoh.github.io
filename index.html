About Me

Hello, my name is Ryan Kohanski and I am currently wrapping up my fourth and final year as an undergraduate student at The University of Texas at Austin. In May 2021 I will graduate with a business degree in Management Information Systems along with coursework relates to computer science and statistics.

Background

I was born in the Sugar Land, TX as a middle child into a family of 5 (I have an older sister and a younger brother). I grew up with an entrepreneurial spirit, starting my own online apparel company in high school. I quickly became fascinated with startups and building/growing a company from scratch, hence my decision to pursue a business degree. Throughout my college career, I fell in love with technology and computer science, and shortly disscovered the field of data science. I loved the idea of being able to tell stories and make decisions based on facts and numbers, as well as the creativity and unexpected findings of such projects. I therefore decided to enroll in classes that would teach me the necessary skills to become a successful data scientist. I have also been involved in multiple projects related to web development, and enjoy deploying my creativity through websites. At UT I served as an Officer for Texas Convergent and the Entrepreneurship Committee Chair for the business fraternity I was in, Phi Gamma Nu.
